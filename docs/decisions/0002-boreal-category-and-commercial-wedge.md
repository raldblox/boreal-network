# 0002 Boreal Category And Commercial Wedge

## Context

The original Boreal Network canon established the durable object model and lifecycle shape, but it did not fully lock the commercial reading of the system.

That left room for drift toward multiple weak interpretations:

- agent infrastructure
- generic work marketplace
- internal copilot suite
- protocol-first network

Those interpretations would confuse pitch work, roadmap choices, and monetization decisions.

## Decision

Boreal's commercial category is:

- `request-native work commerce`

Boreal's system shape remains:

- `request-native work network`

The first commercial wedge is:

- high-value digital work requests from startup and SMB buyers where intake is ambiguous and fulfillment may mix human and AI supply

The front door is:

- external work demand entering as a `Request`

Not:

- internal employee productivity software
- generic agent infrastructure
- agency-led custom delivery as the primary business model

## Consequences

- future pitch and roast work must lead with commerce and fulfillment continuity, not transport or infrastructure
- future product expansion into enterprise, private supply, peer runtimes, or gateways must still preserve the external request-rooted thesis
- `README.md`, `AGENTS.md`, and the commercial canon docs must be updated together if the category or wedge changes
