04 / 12
Key insight

Physical presence is not an edge case.

Plans must preserve onsite checks, local access, pickup, witnessed handoff, and photo proof instead of calling text output complete.

AI planner
human lead
Request
onsite proof
reviewer
Human work can be inserted.

The request can route to a field inspector, operator, reviewer, or local technician when the job requires it.

Generated reports are not proof.

Proof means accepted artifacts, evidence claims, signatures, photos, test results, or owner review.

AI plans should not underwork.

Boreal treats missing access, geography, timing, and safety details as planning truth, not prompt noise.
